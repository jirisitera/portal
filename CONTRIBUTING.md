# Contributing to PortalMod

PortalMod is **our passion project**. We’ve spent years carefully crafting this mod to ensure features meet a specific vision of quality and "vanilla" feel. To ensure the mod stays true to that vision and remains our love child, we manage development very closely.

This means **we are not looking for active contributions of new features or gameplay changes.** We want to keep the creative direction of PortalMod within our team.

## What we welcome
We are happy to accept community contributions such as the following:

- **Bug Fixes:** If you find a bug/issue and know how to fix it, please feel free to submit a PR.
- **Optimization:** Changes that improve performance or clean up internal logic without changing behavior.
- **Quality of Life:** Small, non-intrusive tweaks or quality of life features.

## Guidelines
1.  **Check Issues:** Before fixing a bug/issue, ensure it's documented in the [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/snowy-shack/PortalMod/issues) page. Link it in your PR.
2.  **Stay on Task:** If a Pull Request shifts the design in a way we don't agree with, it will be closed. Please don't take it personally; we just want to keep the project's scope personal to us.
3.  **Test your code:** This mod allows for a lot of opportunity for edge cases to exhibit back-stabbing. Please test your changes thoroughly to ensure it doesn't create more bugs.
4.  **Art changes:** We have a very specific workflow for maintaining our assets. We are open to suggestions on art, but please do so on Discord instead of directly making changes. 

## Want to do more?
If you want to make more in-depth contributions than specified above, please reach out. Send a message to `@phantomeye` on discord, or an email `portalmod.official@gmail.com`. Maybe we could use your help to improve PortalMod.

## Questions?
If you're unsure if a change you have in mind fits these guidelines, please chat with us on our **[Discord Server](https://discord.gg/hUXXtfJc44)**. We’re always happy to talk!
